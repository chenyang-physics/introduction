> About Me

I am a theoretical physicist at Scuola Normale Superiore, affiliated with the Istituto Nazionale di Fisica Nucleare (INFN) Section Pisa.
Originally from China, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Fudan University. I was then a master student at the University of Amsterdam with Diego M. Hofman. I am completing my PhD at Scuola Normale Superiore with Guilherme L. Pimentel and Augusto Sagnotti.

I'm broadly interested in different aspects of theoretical and observational cosmology, particularly on Cosmological Collider Physics and Primordial Cosmology Model Building. I mainly study the mathematical and phenomenological aspects of cosmological correlators. Currently I'm particularly interested in the strongly coupled sectors. I'm also interested in UV completions of inflation models and the data analysis of imprints in the Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) and Large-Scale Structure (LSS).
I'm fortunate to work with a fantastic group of fellow students and postdocs at Scuola Normale.
